Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We have answers.

How much does this service cost?

It is completely free (as in beer). We don't even know who you are, just use it anytime, anywhere.

So, how do you guys make money?

We are a bunch of easy going developers. We already have good jobs, so don't worry. :) Pismail is an effort to make others' lives easier. Besides, "we also hate spam"!

Is Pismail secure?

Pismail is not built for daily messaging. It is only useful for registration forms. Since there is no password needed to check an email account, anybody can come and read your messages. So... use it for cases, where security is not a concern.

Will you share my information with anybody else?

Well... It is impossible. Because we don't even know anything about you. Remember, you didn't register for this service, right?

Some messages are modified and don't display right

Some spam email, contain Javascript and weird CSS attributes. We sanitize the email messages for your protection.

My attachments are missing

Pismail is intended to be used only for registration forms. So, an email with an attachment would be irrelevant for this purpose. We automatically delete the attachments. Sorry...
